Philosophy

Zotero is a tool to aid research and writing. As a result, the UI/UX should be set up to minimize the amount of time the user spends in the app. This is one thing that sets Zotero’s design needs apart from other tools such as Word or Photoshop. The better Zotero is to use, the less time the user will spend in it.
